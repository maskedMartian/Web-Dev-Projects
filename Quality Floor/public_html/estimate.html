Rough Estimate Tool

This Rough Estimate Tool generates a rough cost estimate for a single square or rectangular room using your measurements. Per square yard prices for flooring can be found on our product pricing page. To take measurements for the estimate, measure the room from wall to wall at the widest point in each direction. Round any partial inches up to the nearest inch. If there is a doorway on the wall you are measuring to, measure into the doorway to the center of the spot the door sits when it is closed. Enter your measurements (in feet and inches), the type of flooring, and the per square yard price in the form input boxes below and click the Get Estimate button. The tool will use your measurements to calculate how much product you need and then use that figure along with the per square yard product price you enter and our installation rate to figure an overall rough cost estimate.

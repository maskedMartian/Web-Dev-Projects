Quality Floor was established in 1987 when Doug Mishler and his wife Phyllis bought her father's salvage business and transformed it into an industry authorized floorcoverings dealer. They assembled a showroom with an extensive selection of products from industry-leading manufacturers. They built a large storeroom so they are able to keep a fine selection of products in stock. And, they hired highly skilled professional floorcovering installers to provide the highest quality installations. Since that time Quality Floor has been offering quality products and professional installations at discount prices. All our products and labor are warrantied, and customer satisfaction is our main goal. We want you to enjoy the comfort and beauty of your new floor for years to come because there is nothing we enjoy more than the satisfaction of a job well done and a happy customer. That's why a large section of our customer base are return customers we have been working with for years. In addition to product and installation warranties, and an extensive selection of products, we also offer free measures and estimates and a down home atmosphere. So come in and visit us today! Take a look around our showroom, find something you love, talk to Phyllis or our store manager Lisa, and schedule an appointment to have Doug come out to measure your floor. You won't regret your trip to Quality Floor!
